About Miller's Electrical

Family-owned and operated since 2010. Small enough to care, big enough to handle anything — and never too busy for a knock-off Miller's.

Our story

Built on word of mouth

Miller's Electrical started in 2010 with one ute, one licence, one esky, and a simple idea: if you quote honestly, turn up when you say you will, and leave the job tidy, the phone keeps ringing. Fifteen years and nearly five thousand jobs later, that's still how we operate — knock-off beer optional, but encouraged.

Today we're a team of six licensed electricians and two apprentices covering Perth's southern suburbs — from Bibra Lake to Rockingham and everywhere in between. Most of our work still comes from repeat customers and their referrals, which is exactly how we like it.

We're fully licensed with Building and Energy (WA), carry $20M public liability insurance, and every installation we do is backed by a lifetime workmanship warranty. If something we installed ever fails because of our work, we fix it free. No arguments.

  • Upfront fixed pricing You approve the price before we start. No hourly-rate surprises.
  • Lifetime workmanship warranty If our work fails, we return and fix it at no cost — for as long as you own the property.
  • Licensed & insured WA Electrical Contractor Licence EC 00000 · $20M public liability.
  • Respect for your place Drop sheets down, boots off where asked, and we vacuum before we leave.
