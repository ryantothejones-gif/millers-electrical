Our Services

Every job — big or small — comes with fixed upfront pricing, a compliance certificate where required, and a lifetime warranty on our workmanship.

Residential Electrical

Your home's wiring should be safe, tidy and invisible. We handle everything from small jobs to complete rewires of older brick-and-tile and character homes.

  • Power points, USB outlets & dedicated circuits
  • LED lighting design & installation
  • Switchboard & safety switch upgrades
  • Ceiling fans & bathroom heat-light-fans
  • Full & partial house rewires

Commercial & Office

We keep businesses compliant and powered without disrupting trade — after-hours and staged work is our bread and butter.

  • Shop & office fit-outs
  • Data, phone & network cabling
  • Emergency & exit lighting compliance
  • Test & tag programs
  • Three-phase power & machinery circuits

Solar, Battery & EV Charging

CEC-accredited design and installation sized to your actual usage — we'll show you the payback maths before you commit to anything.

  • Grid-connect solar systems
  • Battery storage & hybrid inverters
  • EV charger supply & install
  • System health checks & repairs

24/7 Emergency Callouts

Electrical emergencies don't keep business hours. One call, and a licensed electrician is on the way — we target a 60-minute response across our service area.

  • Total or partial loss of power
  • Burning smells & buzzing switchboards
  • Constantly tripping safety switches
  • Storm & water damage make-safe

Safety & Compliance Inspections

Whether you're buying, selling or renting out a property, we provide written inspection reports that satisfy insurers, agents and your own peace of mind.

  • WA smoke alarm & RCD compliance (sales & rentals)
  • Safety switch & earthing tests
  • Pre-purchase electrical inspections
  • Rental property compliance certificates
